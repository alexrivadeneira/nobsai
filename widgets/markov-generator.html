How this works (and what it has to do with ChatGPT)

When you click Generate, the page reads your text and builds a table of which word tends to follow each pair of words. For example, after “the White” the next word might be “Rabbit” 3 times out of 3. Then it writes new text by starting somewhere and repeatedly rolling weighted dice to pick each next word.

That is the same core idea as ChatGPT: predict the next word. ChatGPT just uses a neural network trained on trillions of words instead of a small lookup table — which is why it sounds smart and this sounds a little drunk. Notice how this generator produces sentences that flow but don't mean anything reliable. When ChatGPT confidently states something false, you're seeing the polished version of the same phenomenon.
